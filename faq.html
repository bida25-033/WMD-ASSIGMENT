Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, and services.

We specialize in premium human hair wigs, synthetic wigs, and human hair blends. Our collection includes HD lace frontals, body wave wigs, straight bobs, deep wave closures, and vibrant colored styles to suit every personality and budget.

Short wigs (8-12") are perfect for a chic, low-maintenance look. Medium lengths (14-18") offer versatility for everyday wear. Long wigs (20-30") are ideal for dramatic, glamorous styles. Consider your lifestyle and comfort when choosing.

HD lace (High Definition lace) is an ultra-thin, transparent lace material that blends seamlessly with all skin tones. Unlike regular lace, HD lace is virtually invisible when applied, giving you the most natural-looking hairline possible.

With proper care, our human hair wigs can last 1-3 years. We recommend gentle washing with sulfate-free shampoo, deep conditioning, and storing on a wig stand when not in use. Avoid excessive heat styling to prolong the lifespan.

Yes! We offer fast shipping to all major cities and towns in Botswana including Gaborone, Francistown, Maun, and Kasane. Standard delivery takes 2-4 business days. Express delivery is available for urgent orders.

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unused wigs in their original packaging. For hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns on wigs that have been worn, washed, or altered. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Yes! Our human hair wigs can be dyed, curled, straightened, and styled just like your natural hair. We recommend consulting a professional stylist for major color changes to avoid damage. Always use heat protectant when styling.

Use a soft measuring tape to measure around your head from the hairline at your forehead, behind your ears, to the nape of your neck. Most of our wigs come with adjustable straps and combs to ensure a secure, comfortable fit.

Yes, we offer professional wig installation services at our Main Mall boutique (Plot 5, Floor 3). You can book an appointment by calling (267) 77919742 or visiting our Contact page to send a message.

Synthetic wigs require gentle care. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle, wash with cool water and mild shampoo, and air dry on a wig stand. Avoid heat styling unless the wig is specifically labeled as heat-resistant.
